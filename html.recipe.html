Butter Chicken

+

By Usman Arshad. The best butter chicken recipe you ever seen.

+

For the marinated chicken cubes +

    +
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into cubes
    • +
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
    • +
  • 2 teaspoons tandoori masala powder
    • +
  • 2 teaspoons Madras curry powder
    • +
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • +
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
    • +
  • ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
    • +
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
    • +
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
    • +
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
    • +
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • +
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • +
  • 1 cup tomato puree
    • +
  • 1 cup half-and-half
    • +
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
    • +
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, stems removed
    • +
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh ginger
    • +
+ Directions: + +
    +
  1. Mix all the spices together into a bowl
    2. +
  2. Place chicken in a large bowl and add 1/2 the spice mixture; turn to coat evenly.
    3. +
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken; cook and stir until lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat.
    4. +
  4. Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
    5. +
  5. Stir tomato puree into onion mixture and cook, stirring frequently, about 2 minutes. Pour in half-and-half and yogurt. Reduce heat to low and simmer sauce, stirring frequently, about 10 minutes.
    6. +
  6. Pour blended sauce over chicken in the skillet. Simmer until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes. Garnish with cilantro.
    7. +
+

Coming from a brown household, we always had butter chicken weather it was with rice or naan bread. I grew up watching my mom cook it and leanred a little from her and ever since then butter chicken has been my favorite dish to cook and eat.
